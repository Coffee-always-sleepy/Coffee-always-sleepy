President's Message

COLLEGE PRESIDENT

MA. LIBERTY DG. PASCUAL, Ph.D.

Welcome to the OFFICIAL COLLEGE PORTAL OF NORZAGARAY COLLEGE.

NORZAGARAY COLLEGE, with the full support of our Local Government through the leadership of our dear MAYOR MA. ELENA L. GERMAR, has taken this digitization initiative in the ICT Digitization Roadmap of Norzagaray College by launching this portal with the integration of our student's ONLINE ENROLLMENT & ADMISSION MANAGEMENT SYSTEM.

The College has continually strived to become a viable participant in youth & workforce edification and has increased the efforts to assist in that development. At present, we remain true to our goal to make a difference in the lives of thousands of students in a gender-sensitive, safe, and progressive learning environment manned by its competent and committed public servants of teaching and non-teaching personnel.

Exercise of shared governance, development and implementation of significant and relevant programs and projects, active collaboration with internal stakeholders, and sustaining partnerships with external stakeholders, among others -- endeavors and efforts to ensure the holistic development of our learners which can only reach its full worth when we all work together as one.

This College Portal will serve as the information board and academic exchange center that is readily accessible to all, providing faster services and a glimpse of data, programs, projects, and activities of the College. It shall also be an avenue for generation of mutual support within and among the stakeholders for provision of better educational services. Together, we will build a strong learning community with the Students of Norzagaray College as our core.

We welcome your engagement and feedback.
